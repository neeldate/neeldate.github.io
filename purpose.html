Purpose of this website

I want some attention when I do something I'm proud of, but less than and different to that offered by social media.
This website is a way to regulate that attention.
If you'd like to get in touch, my email address is at the bottom of the homepage.

This website is an output of my slow thinking.
When I am asked a question in a conversation, I often can't give an accurate and concise answer because doing so needs more thought.
After having given careful thought to some real questions asked in past conversations, and some I made up myself, some answers live here.
Maybe they'll be useful when I am asked the same questions again, whether in a casual conversation or in a high stakes one where such questions can be traps, like in interviews.

This website challenges me to introspect and convert the notions of my conclusions into words.
I write better than I speak, so I want my real thoughts to be just as tangible as the ones I express via speech.

This website makes it easier to decide what I want to say yes and no to, because it is intended to serve as a reflection of my priorities.
