Make work joyful

You won't grit your way through joyless endeavours.
If pursuing something is important, find a way to make it joyful.

Ask yourself what it would look like if it were fun.
Most times, a simple tweak to the activity or your attitude does the trick.
Other times, you'll find that the activity itself is not authentic to you, and you'll redirect your energy.

As you move forward in your pursuit of choice, make further occasional tweaks to keep the joy intact.
That is how an interest becomes an obsession.
No one can outwork the person that is having fun while doing it.
